Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long do your services typically last?

Our services are designed for longevity. A basic manicure lasts 3-5 days, gel manicures 2-3 weeks, and acrylic sets 3-4 weeks with proper care.

Do you use quality products?

Absolutely! We only use premium, professional-grade products that are safe, durable, and gentle on your nails. All our tools are sterilized between clients.

Can I customize my nail design?

Yes! We specialize in custom designs. Bring your inspiration or work with our artists to create a unique look that expresses your personal style.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance, especially for weekends. For intricate designs, allow extra time for consultation.
